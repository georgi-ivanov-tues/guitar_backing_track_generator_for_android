About The Application

This is a school project created by Georgi Ivanov and Nedelcho Delchev,students in 11-th grade in the "Technological School of Electronic Systems"in Sofia, Bulgaria, for the subject "Technology of Programming" with teacher Kiril Mitov.

The project for android phones will generate backing tracks for guitar for soloing. The app will choose the tracks by the users preferences of key, mode and style. .

The user will also be able to records his playing over the track as well as adding the track to a "favourites" list.
